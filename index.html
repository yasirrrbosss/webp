About SeiGhost

SeiGhost NFT nestled in the Sei network, unveils a captivating collection comprising 3333 enigmatic NFTs. Beyond their aesthetic allure, these NFTs bring a gamut of utility features for holders, chief among them being the innovative NFT staking mechanism.

Through the concept of NFT staking, SeiGhost holders have the opportunity to engage in a dynamic process, where their NFTs serve as a gateway to earning SeiGhost native tokens. This dual-layered functionality not only enhances the intrinsic value of the NFTs but also fosters a sense of active participation within the SeiGhost community.

In a move set to elevate the user experience further, SeiGhost is gearing up to unveil an upcoming gambling (plinko) game. This game promises an immersive blend of entertainment and interactivity, amplifying the thrill for NFT holders. As the anticipation builds, SeiGhost continues to carve its niche as a multifaceted ecosystem, seamlessly merging the worlds of NFTs, staking, and gaming on the Sei network.
